Quick Info

Background Information

The goal of the organization is to immerse highly motivated students in a community where they can explore their academic passions, and provide them with opportunites both academically and financially to become life-time thinkers and leaders.

How To Apply - Rising Freshmen

Incoming freshmen cadets, identified by their academic and extracurricfular activites on their Citadel application paperwork, may be contacted by the Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships and invited to apply to be a member of the 1842 Scholars Program.

The Leadership Council

Lead by the elected President, and made up of the Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer. The Leadership Council is responsible for the creation, maintance, and enaction of the 1842 Articles of Organization and maintaining a legacy of excellency.

The Bling

1842 Scholars, along with any other benefits of being a member of the 1842 Scholars Program, will recieve a 1842 Honor Program Patch for their Field Jacket along with a 1842 Honor Ribbon for their Leave Uniform.
